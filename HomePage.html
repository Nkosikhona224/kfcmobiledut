About Us

Yum!'s subsidiary KFC Brands, Inc. A global brand of chicken restaurants with decades of success and innovation is Yum! (NYSE: YUM). Everything began with one cook, Colonel Harland Sanders, who made a finger lickin' great recipe over quite a while back, a rundown of mystery spices and flavors fixed on the rear of the way to his kitchen. In over 26,000 restaurants in over 150 countries and territories around the world, we still adhere to his formula for success, with real cooks breading and freshly preparing our delicious chicken by hand.
